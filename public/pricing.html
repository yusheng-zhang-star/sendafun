Frequently Asked Questions

→ What is your refund policy?
No partial refunds on one-time cards. Subscriptions are cancellable at the end of the current billing period per our Terms of Service §4. Pay-per-send purchases are non-refundable once the card has been delivered to the recipient. Annual plans may be eligible for a pro-rata refund within 14 days of purchase if unused.
→ Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through Creem checkout, with PCI DSS Level 1 compliance and end-to-end encryption for every transaction. No card data is stored on our servers.
→ How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel any time directly from the confirmation email sent when you subscribed, or by emailing support@sendafun.com with your order reference. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle, and you will retain access until that date. There are no cancellation fees or penalties.
→ Can I gift a subscription to someone else?
Yes! The Gift subscription feature is included in the Monthly Unlimited, Annual Unlimited, and Group Card Pass plans. You can purchase 1-month, 3-month, 6-month, or 12-month gift codes that the recipient can redeem at any time. Gift codes are delivered instantly by email.
→ Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
We offer a 30% discount for registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits, K-12 schools, and accredited universities. Contact enterprise@sendafun.com with your organization details and we will set up a custom discount code within one business day. Bulk pricing for 100+ cards per month is also available.

💱 All transactions are finalised in USD (US Dollars) through our secure Creem checkout. Local currency prices are displayed as estimates only, calculated using the exchange rate snapshot available at the edge. The exact amount charged by your card issuer may vary slightly depending on the foreign exchange rate at the time of settlement. No exchange rate lock-in is provided.
