How B2B bulk sending works

  1. Pick an occasion template, open the brand kit, and set your logo, primary color, and custom reply-to email.
  2. Upload a CSV with columns Name, Email, Language, Department, Date — we auto-personalise each card.
  3. Schedule or send now; after delivery, download the analytics CSV (open rate, click, reply count).

B2B & white-label features

  • 📄 CSV import wizard — drag & drop spreadsheet; auto-detect columns, merge-tag preview per row
  • 🎨 Brand kit locker — save logo, HEX colors, default fonts, signature footer, reply-to address
  • 🌐 White-label domain — cards served from cards.yourcompany.com instead of sendafun.com
  • 📊 Analytics CSV export — opens, clicks, time-on-card, replies and bounces by recipient
  • 👥 Sub-user seats — add team members (HR, comms, office managers) to send under the same brand
