How GIF & video export works

  1. Design your card, then open the effects panel to pick 1–6 decor layers (confetti, snow, hearts, etc).
  2. Preview the animation timeline live in the editor — adjust speed, density and particle colors.
  3. Hit export; CCapture.js renders the canvas locally and downloads a GIF or MP4 at your chosen resolution.
