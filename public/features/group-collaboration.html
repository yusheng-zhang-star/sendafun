How group collaboration works

  1. Start a template, add your message and photos, then generate a unique shareable invite link.
  2. Send the link via Slack, WhatsApp, email or text — anyone with the link can add their signature.
  3. Lock the deadline (or lock manually) once everyone has signed, then deliver to the recipient.

Core collaboration features

  • 🔗 Magic invite link — no login required for co-signers, just open and sign
  • ⏰ Deadline auto-lock — signatures freeze automatically when the date hits
  • 🔒 Manual lock override — admin can lock the card early at any time
  • 📧 Email nudges — gentle reminders sent to pending signers 48h before deadline
  • 👑 Admin invite list — pre-load recipients and track who has / hasn't signed
