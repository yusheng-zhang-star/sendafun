Privacy Policy

Overview

SendAFun Ltd. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the sendafun.com website and the SendAFun 2.0 digital greeting card service (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website, create an account, purchase a plan, or send a greeting card through our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Isle of Man Data Protection Act 2002. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

We process personal data only for the purposes described in this policy and on the legal bases of consent, contract performance, legitimate interest, and legal obligation, as applicable. We encourage you to read this policy carefully and contact us at privacy@sendafun.com with any questions regarding our data practices. This policy applies to all visitors, users, customers, and other individuals who access or use the Service.

We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time in accordance with Section 10 below. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We will notify you of material changes by email (if you have an account) or by posting a prominent notice on our homepage prior to the change becoming effective.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the Service. This includes: your name and email address when you create an account or send a card; the recipient's name and email address when you schedule a delivery; payment information (processed by our payment provider Creem and not stored on our servers); your message content, signature, and card customizations; and any correspondence you send to our support or privacy teams. We also collect information about the templates you browse, save, and send to help improve our recommendation engine.

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies. This includes: your IP address, device type, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent on pages, the date and time of your visit, and other diagnostic data. We also collect information about how you interact with our card editor, including clicks, scrolls, and feature usage patterns. This data is collected and processed in aggregate form wherever possible.

If you use our Group Signatures feature, we collect the names, email addresses, and signatures of each group participant who joins your card. This information is provided either by the card organizer or by the participants themselves when they follow the invitation link. Participants may request deletion of their signature and associated personal data at any time by contacting privacy@sendafun.com with the card reference number.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes: to provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including delivering cards at the scheduled date and time; to process payments, issue receipts, and manage your subscription plan; to create and authenticate your user account; to send you transactional emails including confirmations, delivery notifications, password resets, and support responses; to personalize your experience and recommend templates based on your usage history; and to detect, prevent, and investigate fraudulent transactions, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities.

We also use your data for our legitimate business interests, including: analyzing usage trends and performance metrics to optimize the platform; conducting A/B testing and product research to develop new features; monitoring the uptime, security, and stability of our infrastructure; and enforcing our Terms of Service and other legal agreements. Where we rely on legitimate interest as a legal basis, we ensure that such interest is not overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms by conducting a data protection impact assessment for high-risk processing activities.

With your explicit consent, we may also use your email address to send promotional communications including new feature announcements, seasonal template releases, and special offers. You may withdraw your consent to marketing communications at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email or by updating your preferences in your account settings. Promotional emails will never exceed a frequency of four messages per calendar month.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (including web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage) to enhance your experience on the Service. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and enable us to recognize you across pages and sessions, remember your preferences, and keep you logged in. For a detailed description of the individual cookies we use and your options to control them, please refer to our separate Cookies & Tracking Policy at /cookies.html, which is incorporated by reference into this Privacy Policy.

We categorize cookies into three broad groups: Essential cookies are required for the basic operation of the Service and cannot be disabled; they include session cookies, CSRF tokens, and load balancer identifiers. Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website; we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with IP anonymization enabled for this purpose. Advertising cookies allow our advertising partners to serve personalized ads across the web; these are only set with your explicit consent via our cookie consent banner.

You can manage and delete cookies through your browser or device settings. Please note that deleting or blocking certain cookies may impair the functionality of the Service, particularly features that require you to remain logged in or that depend on saved preferences. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, which is available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. User account data (name, email, hashed password, billing history) is retained for the duration of your active account plus 36 months after closure or inactivity, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law. Deleted accounts are marked for soft deletion immediately, and all associated data is permanently purged from our active databases within 90 days of your deletion request.

Card content, including messages, signatures, and template selections, is retained for a minimum of 12 months after the scheduled delivery date to allow for re-delivery requests, historical access, and support investigations. After this period, cards are moved to cold storage for an additional 24 months before permanent deletion. You may request earlier deletion of any specific card by contacting support@sendafun.com with the card reference number, provided that the card has already been delivered or that the scheduled delivery date has not yet passed.

Payment transaction records are retained in accordance with applicable tax and financial regulations: a minimum of 7 years from the date of the transaction for VAT and accounting purposes. These records are processed and stored by our payment processor Creem; we retain only the transaction ID, amount, date, and last four digits of the payment method. Email correspondence with our support and privacy teams is retained for 36 months from the date of resolution, after which tickets are anonymized or permanently deleted. Server and CDN access logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

6

Third-Party Services

We use a carefully selected set of third-party service providers to deliver the Service, and each provider processes personal data only under written agreements that include adequate data protection safeguards. Our core infrastructure providers include Cloudflare for CDN, DNS, and DDoS protection (with access to visitor IP addresses and request metadata); Cloudflare R2 for object storage of card template assets and rendered card images (encrypted at rest with server-side AES-256); and our primary hosting provider for application servers and managed database services.

Payment processing is handled by Creem, a registered Payment Institution. When you make a purchase, your payment information is submitted directly to Creem via an embedded checkout iframe and never touches our application servers. Creem's processing of your payment data is governed by Creem's own privacy policy and PCI DSS Level 1 compliance program. We receive from Creem only the minimum information necessary to fulfill your order: transaction ID, amount, currency, last four card digits, and billing postal code.

Transactional and marketing email is delivered via Resend, with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication enabled on all outgoing domains. Anonymous web analytics and conversion attribution are handled by Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with IP anonymization and cookieless pings enabled on EU traffic. Advertising and remarketing are handled through Google AdSense and Google Ads, subject to your consent on the cookie banner. All third-party processors have been vetted for compliance with GDPR Article 28 requirements and appropriate Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are in place where required.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights under applicable data protection law: the right to access a copy of the personal data we hold about you and our processing activities; the right to request rectification of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data; the right to request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to our legal retention obligations; the right to restrict processing of your personal data under certain conditions; the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing purposes; and the right to data portability in machine-readable format for data you provided to us.

If we are processing your personal data on the basis of your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal. To exercise any of these rights, please send a signed and dated request to privacy@sendafun.com with the subject line "Data Subject Request". We will respond to valid requests within 30 calendar days, extendable by an additional 60 days for complex or high-volume requests. We may ask you to provide proof of identity to prevent unauthorized disclosure of your personal data.

If you are a resident of California under the CCPA, you have the additional right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes. You can exercise this right by clicking "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" in the footer of our website or by emailing privacy@sendafun.com. We do not and will not sell the personal data of minors under 16 years of age. If you believe our processing of your personal data violates applicable law, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority, including the Isle of Man Information Commissioner (www.infocommissioner.org.im).

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Service is intended for use by individuals who are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or process personal information from children under the age of 13, and no part of the Service is designed to attract children under 13. In compliance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), if we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will immediately delete that information from our servers without notice.

If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe that your child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@sendafun.com with the subject line "COPPA Request". We will respond within 10 business days and take all commercially reasonable steps to permanently delete the child's personal data from our active and backup systems. We also recommend that parents and guardians supervise their children's online activities and consider using parental control tools to ensure a safe online experience.

For users between the ages of 13 and 17, we require that a parent or legal guardian review and accept these Terms and our Privacy Policy on behalf of the minor before the minor creates an account or makes any purchase. Users under 18 are not permitted to enter into paid subscriptions or one-time purchases without parental authorization, and we reserve the right to cancel any such transactions if we have reason to believe that parental consent was not properly obtained.

International Data Transfers

The Service is operated from our offices in the Isle of Man, and the personal data we collect is primarily processed in data centers located within the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom. However, some of our third-party service providers may transfer and process your personal data in countries outside of your jurisdiction of residence, including the United States, Canada, and Singapore, which may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. Wherever your personal data is transferred, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your rights and freedoms.

For transfers of personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to countries not recognized as having an adequate level of data protection by the European Commission or the UK Secretary of State (as applicable), we rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), Module 2 (controller-to-processor), as approved under Decision (EU) 2021/914, supplemented by appropriate technical and organizational security measures. A copy of our executed SCCs with each relevant sub-processor is available on request by emailing privacy@sendafun.com.

We conduct annual transfer impact assessments for all cross-border data flows to document our rationale and verify that the laws and practices of the recipient country do not impair the effectiveness of the contractual safeguards. All data in transit between our application and third-party processors is encrypted at minimum TLS 1.3, and data at rest is encrypted with AES-256 or equivalent. If you would like further information about the specific countries in which your personal data is processed, please contact our privacy team.
