About SendAFun 2.0

"We believe every card should feel like a group hug from everyone who cares — built in 3 clicks, delivered with love."

Our Mission

SendAFun started as a small weekend project in 2024 with a simple, stubborn idea: sending a heartfelt digital card should never feel like filling out a spreadsheet. Two years, thousands of user conversations, and hundreds of millions of delivered smiles later, that same belief still anchors every product decision we make. We exist to make the act of remembering someone's birthday, a graduation, a promotion, a quiet "I miss you," or a tough "get well soon" as effortless as it is meaningful. No ads, no dark patterns, no locked features you'll never use — just a beautiful, honest tool for showing up.

We're committed to celebrating the small, ordinary moments that bind families, friend groups, remote teams, and communities together. Every feature we ship is designed around the question: "Would we use this ourselves, for someone we actually love?" If the answer isn't an immediate yes, it doesn't ship. That constraint has guided us from version 1.0 to 2.0 and will continue to guide us into the future.

What's New in 2.0

The biggest, most-requested rebuild of SendAFun ever. A few things we're especially proud of:

🎯 Intent Engine 👥 Group Signatures 🎁 Gift-a-Month ✨ Glass UI 🗂️ 250+ Templates ⚡ R2 CDN 📅 Smart Scheduling 🔐 No account required
🎯

Intent Engine — 3-click discovery

Tell us who it's for, the vibe, and a sentence about them. Our new recommendation engine surfaces 12 perfectly-matched templates instead of making you scroll through 250. No more decision fatigue.

👥

Group Signatures

Collect 5, 50, or 500 signatures from the whole team, family, or class with a single shareable link. Signers can add short notes, emoji, and a doodle without creating an account. Everyone sees the full card before it sends.

🎁

Gift-a-Month subscriptions

Buy a 1, 3, 6, or 12-month gift code for a friend who sends a lot of cards (or for a teacher, volunteer, or parent who deserves one less thing to think about). Redeemable anytime, no expiry.

✨

Glass UI design system

A complete visual rebuild with soft gradients, blurred backdrops, and generous whitespace. Optimized for 5G and slow connections alike, with every template asset served from Cloudflare's R2 edge CDN in 285+ locations.

🗂️

250 templates on R2 CDN

From anniversaries to zero-waste congratulations, our in-house design team plus 40+ independent illustrators now contribute to a fully royalty-free, CC0-compatible template library. New designs every Monday.

📅

Smart scheduling & delivery

Pick a date and time, we handle the rest — with timezone-aware delivery, automatic retry for bounces, and a re-send button you can actually find. You'll also get a confirmation email and a delivery receipt.

Our Team

SendAFun is built by a small, fully remote-first team of Product, Design, and Engineering humans distributed across 6 time zones. We don't have an office — we have a shared playlist, a weekly check-in on Fridays, and a very opinionated Notion. Everyone on the team sends at least 4 cards a month to friends and family; dogfooding isn't a process, it's how we know what to build next.

We've been lucky to work with dozens of freelance illustrators, writers, and accessibility consultants across two years of SendAFun.
